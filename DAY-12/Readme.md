# Day 12 – AI Agents, FinLo Parking System, and Digital Marketing

**Date:** 9th July 2026

---

## Overview

The twelfth day of the Agentic AI Training focused on the practical implementation of AI Agents and their significance in addressing real-world challenges. The session explained how AI agents gather information from their surroundings, analyze data, make intelligent decisions, and execute actions autonomously. A practical case study on FinLo, an AI-powered parking management system, showcased how artificial intelligence can improve parking operations in shopping malls and public spaces. Additionally, the session introduced the fundamentals of Digital Marketing, emphasizing its role, major channels, and effective strategies for promoting products and services online.
